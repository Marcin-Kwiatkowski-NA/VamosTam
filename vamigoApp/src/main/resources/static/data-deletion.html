Data Deletion Instructions

Vamigo — Ride-Sharing App

Effective date: 1 March 2026

This page explains how to permanently delete your Vamigo account and all personal data associated with it. You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time under the GDPR and our Privacy Policy.

1. Delete Your Account From the App

The fastest way to delete your account and data is directly from the Vamigo mobile app:

  1. Open the Vamigo app and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings (profile icon in the bottom navigation).
  3. Tap Account.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Your account will be deleted immediately. You will be signed out and all associated data (see Section 3) will be removed from our systems.

2. Delete Your Account by Email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request deletion by contacting us directly.

Send an email to: support@vamigo.app

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include: the email address associated with your Vamigo account and confirmation that you wish to delete your account.

For security, we may ask you to verify your identity from the email address on file before processing the request. We will complete deletion within 30 days of receiving a verified request.

3. What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  • Your profile data (name, email, phone number, avatar, bio)
  • Your authentication credentials and linked Google account data
  • All rides and seat requests you created
  • All bookings where you were a passenger
  • All your conversations and messages
  • All your vehicles
  • All your notifications
  • All email verification tokens
  • All registered device (push notification) tokens

4. What May Be Retained

For community safety, moderation, and legal compliance, a small subset of data may be retained in anonymized form after account deletion:

  • Published reviews you authored about other users may be retained without your identifying data.
  • Moderation reports you filed or that were filed against you may be retained for the period required by applicable law.

These retained records do not contain your name, email, or profile details after deletion.

5. Signing In With Google

If you signed in with Google, deleting your Vamigo account also removes the link between your Google account and Vamigo. We do not retain your Google identifier after deletion.

Deleting your Vamigo account does not delete your Google account itself. To manage app connections on the Google side, visit Google Account – Third-party apps.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about data deletion or need help with the process, please contact us:

  • Email: support@vamigo.app
  • Website: https://vamigo.app

See our Privacy Policy for the full list of your rights under the GDPR, including access, rectification, portability, and restriction of processing.
